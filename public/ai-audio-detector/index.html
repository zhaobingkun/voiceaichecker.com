An AI audio detector is useful when the question is about an audio file as a whole: a voiceover, narration clip, podcast segment, or short message. For best results, test a clean speech-only sample rather than music, background noise, or overlapping speakers.

When to Use This AI Audio Detector

Narration and voiceovers

Screen clips from videos, ads, and social posts when the voice sounds polished or synthetic.

Short speech files

Use 10-30 seconds of clear speech. Avoid long files where only part of the clip contains the suspicious voice.

Review before publishing

Use the result as a review signal before trusting, quoting, or reposting an audio clip.
